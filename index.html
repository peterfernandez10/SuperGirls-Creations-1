Our Story

A love for yarn that grew into SuperGirls Creations

It started with a single hook, a ball of pink yarn, and a lot of love. Teericka Malon has always found peace in the rhythm of crochet — the quiet click of the hook, the way a pile of yarn slowly becomes something beautiful.

What began as a cozy hobby turned into SuperGirls Creations: a small studio dedicated to handmade bags, blankets, amigurumi, and wearables — each one crocheted stitch by stitch, just for you.

No mass production, no shortcuts. Just genuine handmade craft, made by a girl who truly loves crochet.

Teericka MalonFounder & Lead Maker
